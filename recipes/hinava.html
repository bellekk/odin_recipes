Hinava Recipe

Description

It is a raw fish salad, so you need to take extra caution on the freshness of your fish, to prevent getting an upset stomach from salmonella, that is.

The main character for this recipe is the mackerel fish, but shark, fil squid and shrimp can also be used as an alternative.

Ingredients

  • 600 grams of raw mackerel or tuna, thinly sliced
  • 125 millilitres of lime juice
  • 200 grams of bitter gourd, halved, seeded and finely sliced
  • 50 grams of ginger, grated into fine strips
  • 100 grams of peeled shallots, finely sliced
  • 8 bird's eye chillies, finely sliced
  • Half teaspoon of salt
  • 4 tablespoons of dried bambangan seeds, grated

Steps

  1. Put the fish slices together with lime juice and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix the mixture using your fingers.
  3. Place the mixing bowl in the refrigerator for 10 minutes to let it marinate.
  4. Once 10 minutes is up, put in the other ingredients into the mixing bowl.
  5. Toss all the ingredients well and serve.
