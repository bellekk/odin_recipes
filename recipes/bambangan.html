Nonsom Bambangan Recipe

Description

When peeling the skin of this particular fruit, you have to be very careful not to let the liquid from the tip to stain the fruit or your bare hand. The liquid will cause a whole other world of itchiness. It is recommended to use a glove or wrap your hand with a plastic bag while peeling the skin. Also, bear in mind not to wash the fruit after peeling the skin off to prevent the fruit from becoming dark in colour during the pickling process.

For this recipe, we need to separate the fruit and its seed, an important ingredient for the pickling process.

Ingredients

  • Ripe bambangan fruits
  • Bambangan seed
  • Salt
  • Chillies (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the yellow pulp into smaller chunks and put it in a bowl.
  2. Grate its white seed on top of the bowl unto the fruit.
  3. Add in salt according to your taste.
  4. Mix well with spoon.
  5. Add in some chillies. (optional)
  6. Transfer everything into an air-tight jar.
  7. Leave it to ferment for a week.
